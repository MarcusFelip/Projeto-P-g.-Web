What services are offered by the petlife clinic?

The petlife clinic offers a wide range of services, including routine consultations, vaccinations, surgeries, treatment of illnesses, dental care, 24-hour emergency care, a flea, tick and worm prevention program, among others. Our goal is to provide comprehensive, personalized care to ensure your pet's health and well-being.

What species of animals does the veterinary clinic serve?

The petlife veterinary clinic treats pets of all species, including dogs and cats, birds, rodents and reptiles. Our professionals have the knowledge and experience to care for different types of animals, offering specialized and dedicated service to each one of them.

Does the petlife clinic have emergency services?

Yes, the petlife clinic offers 24-hour emergency services. If your pet needs immediate veterinary care outside of office hours, our team is ready to help, providing urgent care and appropriate treatment to ensure your pet's well-being.

The clinic offers bathing and grooming services?

Yes, the petlife veterinary clinic offers professional bathing and grooming services. Our team of experienced professionals ensures that your pet receives adequate care, using high quality products and safe techniques. Bathing and grooming help maintain hygiene, the health of your pet's skin and coat, as well as providing a relaxing and pleasant experience for them.
